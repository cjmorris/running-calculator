Training made easy!

Pace calculator

Need help figuring out your pace for a particular distance or race? RunningCalculator's pace calculator makes it easy. Simply enter the distance you're running and the time you want to complete it in, and RunningCalculator will tell you what pace you need to maintain to reach your goal.

Heart Rate Calculator

RunningCalculator's heart rate calculator helps you determine your target heart rate zones based on your lactate threshold. By staying within your target heart rate zone, you can ensure that you're training at the right intensity to achieve your goals.

Training Plans

RunningCalculator offers a variety of training plans to help you achieve your running goals, whether you're looking for a light 5K plan or completing a marathon. The plans are customizable based on your experience level, time frame and goals, and include detailed workout schedules based on your target heart rate zones.
